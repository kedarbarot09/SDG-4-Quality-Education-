How This Project Connects to SDG 4

SDG 4 promotes inclusive and equitable quality education. This project supports its digital goal.

Digital Aspect of SDG 4 & This Project

  • Digital literacy is essential for inclusive, equitable, and quality education as envisioned in SDG 4.
  • It equips learners and educators with the skills to effectively use technology, enabling access to better educational resources.
  • Digital literacy platforms help overcome barriers such as limited access to information and the digital divide.
  • These platforms provide structured IT training that supports literacy, numeracy, and lifelong learning goals.
  • Digital skills improve educational outcomes and prepare learners for employment in the digital economy.
  • Teacher training in digital skills amplifies the impact by enabling quality technology-enabled teaching.
  • Without adequate digital literacy, educational inequalities widen — conflicting with SDG 4’s goal to leave no one behind.
